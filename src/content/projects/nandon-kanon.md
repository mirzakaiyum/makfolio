## Overview

Nandon Kanon is a pristine botanical garden that needed a digital presence as beautiful as its physical grounds. 

## The Build

Built entirely statically using Astro for lightning-fast load times, combined with a headless CMS to allow park rangers to easily update the seasonal blooming calendar. The site incorporates fluid scrolling and immersive full-screen imagery to capture the essence of nature.
