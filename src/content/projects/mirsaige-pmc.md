## Overview

Mirsaige PMC provides construction firms with a God-eye view over their operations, from asset utilization to worker deployment across multiple global sites. 

## Key Features

- **Resource Allocation**: Drag-and-drop scheduling of machinery and personnel.
- **Cost Tracking**: Real-time budget analytics vs projected spend.
- **Reporting**: Automated compliance and progress reporting generation.
